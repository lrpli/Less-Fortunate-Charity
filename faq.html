About Less Fortunate Charity

What is Less Fortunate Charity?

+

Less Fortunate Charity is a 501(c)(3) registered nonprofit organization dedicated to serving the Chicago community. We provide comprehensive support to vulnerable individuals and families through education programs, emergency assistance, job training, and health resources.

What is your organization's EIN?

+

Our EIN is 99-3459999. You can verify our nonprofit status and access our IRS documentation through the IRS Tax Exempt Organization Search.

Who founded Less Fortunate Charity?

+

Less Fortunate Charity was founded with a mission to serve the Chicago community. It is led by our Principal Officer, Patrickson Durand, who shares our commitment to creating positive change.

Where is your organization based?

+

We are located at 2132 E 72nd St, Chicago, IL 60649. Our office is open Monday-Friday 9 AM - 5 PM CST, and Saturday 10 AM - 2 PM CST by appointment.

Programs & Services

What programs do you offer?

+

We offer five main programs: Emergency Assistance (food, shelter, utilities support), Education Support (scholarships, tutoring), Job Training & Career Development, Health & Wellness Resources, and Volunteer Opportunities. Visit our Programs page for detailed information.

How do I apply for emergency assistance?

+

To apply for emergency assistance, contact us at info@LESSFORTUNATE.us or call (312) 555-0100. We'll discuss your situation and guide you through the application process. Emergency applications are prioritized.

What are the eligibility requirements for programs?

+

Eligibility varies by program. Generally, we serve Chicago residents with demonstrated financial need. Some programs have specific requirements (e.g., education programs serve K-12 students and high school seniors). Contact us for specific eligibility details.

How long does the application process take?

+

Application processing times vary by program. Emergency assistance applications are typically processed within 24-48 hours. Educational and job training applications may take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a timeline when you apply.

Donations & Giving

Is my donation tax-deductible?

+

Yes! Since we are a 501(c)(3) registered nonprofit organization (EIN: 99-3459999), all monetary donations are tax-deductible. You'll receive a tax receipt for your records.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and checks. Visit our Donate page for all available giving options.

Can I set up a recurring monthly donation?

+

Absolutely! Monthly recurring donations are a wonderful way to provide consistent support. You can set up a monthly donation through our secure donation form and cancel anytime.

How much of my donation goes to programs?

+

90% of all donations go directly to programs and services. The remaining 10% supports essential operational costs like staffing, facilities, and administrative functions.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

+

Yes! We accept donations of supplies, clothing, furniture, and other items. Contact us at info@LESSFORTUNATE.us to arrange donation drop-off.

Volunteering

How can I volunteer?

+

Interested in volunteering? Contact us at info@LESSFORTUNATE.us or call (312) 555-0100. We have opportunities for program assistants, tutors, mentors, event organizers, and more.

What volunteer opportunities are available?

+

We need volunteers for: Program Assistants, Tutors & Mentors, Event Organizers, Administrative Support, Fundraising, and Community Outreach. Visit our Programs page for more details.

Do I need prior experience to volunteer?

+

No prior experience is necessary! We welcome anyone with a passion for helping others. We provide training and support for all volunteer roles.

How much time do volunteers need to commit?

+

Volunteer commitments are flexible! Whether you can give a few hours a month or several hours a week, we have opportunities to match your availability. Discuss your schedule when you apply.

General Questions

How can I stay updated on your work?

+

Subscribe to our newsletter for monthly updates, impact stories, and program announcements. Follow us on social media (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn) or visit our Blog page.

Does your organization hold events?

+

Yes! We host community events, fundraisers, educational workshops, and volunteer appreciation events throughout the year. Follow our Blog or subscribe to our newsletter for event announcements.

Is my privacy protected when contacting you?

+

Absolutely. We respect your privacy and maintain strict confidentiality for all inquiries and personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I reference your organization in grants or funding?

+

Yes! We welcome partnerships and collaborations. For corporate partnerships or to discuss funding opportunities, contact us at info@LESSFORTUNATE.us.
