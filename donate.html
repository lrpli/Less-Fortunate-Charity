Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Less Fortunate Charity is a 501(c)(3) registered nonprofit organization (EIN: 99-3459999). All donations are tax-deductible. You'll receive a tax receipt via email.

How is my donation used?

90% of donations go directly to programs and services. The remaining 10% supports operational costs. We provide quarterly impact reports detailing how funds are used.

Can I cancel a recurring donation?

Yes! You can cancel anytime by contacting us at info@LESSFORTUNATE.us or managing your subscription.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and PCI-compliant payment processors. Your information is never stored on our servers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Check with us for other payment options.

Have more questions? Contact us or visit our FAQ page.
