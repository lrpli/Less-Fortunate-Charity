Who We Are

Less Fortunate Charity is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to serving the community and supporting those in need. Based in Chicago, Illinois, we work tirelessly to create positive change and improve the lives of disadvantaged individuals and families.

Since our founding, we have touched over 500 lives and supported 100+ families through our comprehensive programs and compassionate services. Our organization is led by Patrickson Durand, our Principal Officer, who shares our vision of a more equitable and compassionate community.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To provide comprehensive support, resources, and opportunities to less fortunate members of our community, empowering them to build better lives and achieve their full potential.

🌟 Our Vision

A compassionate and equitable community where everyone has access to the support they need to thrive, where no one is left behind, and where opportunity is available to all.

❤️ Our Values

Compassion, integrity, inclusivity, and transparency guide our work. We believe in the dignity of every person and the power of community support.
